Privacy Policy

1. Purpose and Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how MarriageGate ("we", "our", "us"), a company registered in Switzerland under the name MGatae, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data of users ("you", "your") of https://marriagegate.com (the "Platform").

It applies to all interactions with the Platform, including website use, subscriptions, and communications.

2. Data Controller and Contact Information

Data Controller: MarriageGate (MGatae)
Jurisdiction: Switzerland
Contact Email: privacymarriagegate.com

For EU users: MarriageGate acts as the Data Controller under Article 4(7) GDPR.

For Swiss users: MarriageGate complies with Art. 5 et seq. of the Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP).

3. Categories of Personal Data Collected

MarriageGate may collect and process the following categories:

Category Examples Legal Basis
Account Data Name, email address, password Contract performance (GDPR Art. 6 (1)(b))
Profile Data Gender, age, location, religion, preferences, biographical details, photos Consent (GDPR Art. 6 (1)(a))
Usage Data Log files, IP address, browser type, device info Legitimate interest (site security & analytics) Art. 6 (1)(f)
Payment Data Billing name, transaction ID (handled by Stripe) Contract performance Art. 6 (1)(b)
Support Data Emails or forms submitted to support Legitimate interest Art. 6 (1)(f)

Important: Sensitive personal data (e.g., religious affiliation or caste information) is processed only with explicit user consent.

4. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is used for:

  • User registration and account management
  • Profile display and matchmaking services
  • Secure communication between users
  • Subscription management and payment processing via Stripe
  • Email notifications, updates, and system alerts
  • Improving Platform performance and security
  • Compliance with legal obligations

MarriageGate does not use personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal effects within the meaning of Article 22 GDPR.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is based on:

  • User consent (for sensitive and optional data)
  • Performance of a contract (for subscriptions and services)
  • Legitimate interests (for site operation and fraud prevention)
  • Legal obligations (for tax and financial record-keeping)

Users may withdraw consent at any time by contacting privacymarriagegate.com.

6. Data Storage and Retention

All data is stored securely on Microsoft Azure servers located in the European region. MarriageGate maintains a centralized database and does not transfer personal data outside this region.

Data retention periods:

  • Active accounts → while the user maintains membership
  • Archived or inactive accounts → retained for legitimate business purposes before anonymization or deletion
  • Financial records → minimum 10 years as required by Swiss commercial law

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

MarriageGate does not sell or rent personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Stripe – for payment processing (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Technical service providers – for hosting and maintenance under confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities – if required by law or court order

8. International Data Transfers

MarriageGate does not transfer data outside the EU or Switzerland. If such transfers become necessary in future, they will only occur in accordance with Art. 16 FADP and Art. 44 et seq. GDPR using adequate safeguards (e.g., SCCs).

9. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits and backups
  • Incident response procedures

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure, and users acknowledge the inherent risks of internet communications.

10. User Rights

Under GDPR and FADP, users have the following rights:

  • Access to personal data
  • Rectification of inaccuracies
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time

Requests may be submitted to privacymarriagegate.com. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

11. Cookies and Tracking

MarriageGate uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain sessions and user preferences
  • Analyze traffic and performance
  • Enhance user experience

Users will receive a cookie consent banner upon first visit to the Platform. For details and choices, see our separate Cookie Policy.

12. Automated Matching Algorithms

MarriageGate uses automated matching algorithms to suggest compatible profiles based on user inputs and preferences. These algorithms do not make binding decisions and are designed solely to enhance user experience.

13. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for adults (18 years and above). We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

14. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal-data breach, MarriageGate will notify the relevant authorities (FDPIC and/or EU DPA) and affected users within the timeframes required by law.
